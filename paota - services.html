Every appointment at Cute Cut begins with complimentary beverage service and professional consultation. Not only will your stylist suggest a cut that will compliment you, but they will also advise how a cut will look on you and how it will grow.

Our stylists are trained to work with all hair types using only the best quality tools, sheers, and scissors for precision. Whether your hair is too fine, too short, too thin, or too damaged, we encourage you to let one of our stylists suggest a hairstyle that will compliment your style and lifestyle. They can accommodate angled cuts, stacked cuts, layered cuts, or blunt cuts, executed with precision and care.
