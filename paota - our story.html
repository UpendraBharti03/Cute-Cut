Cute Cut | PAOTA - Our Story

From the genesis of three stylists, a manicurist, and massage therapist, Cute Cut opened its doors May of 2011 to an overwhelming reception as Birmingham Alabama's premier Aveda Lifestyle Salon where transcendent style and beauty beyond the tradition are forged for all. Cute Cut’s approach towards hair, makeup, and spa services are emulated and framed against a backdrop of clean-lined, industrial decor where new and unique creations are crafted, honed, and inspired by you, for you.

At Cute Cut we strive to reach beyond the roots (of hair), and into the refinement and healing of one’s core self.

- Upendra Bharti , Founder Cute Cut

What makes Cute Cut an Aveda Lifestyle Salon is our exclusive selection of Aveda hair, body, and skin care products, all botanically based and produced with a goal of preserving the environment. Our services include Aveda Hair Color, and Aveda Trend Hair cutting and styling, as well as signature spa treatments and complimentary sensory rituals. Cute Cut is a proud participant in Aveda’s Pure Privilege program, a loyalty program rewarding members who use Aveda products.
